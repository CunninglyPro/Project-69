Campus Life

school ground

was established in 1927

The school ground is where we go in PE period. We play games like cricket, football, badminton etc. We even do our school assembly over here. Sometimes when fire drill occurs in the school, we go in the ground to keep ourselves safe.

school library

was made in 2001

The school library is where we go in Library period. We read books and we can also do our classwork or homework there. There are many books like Harry Potter, Anna Karenina, To Kill a Mockingbird etc.

computer lab

was made in 2003

The computer lab is wher we go in Computer Practical period. We practice what we had learnt in the computer class. We also sometimes make PPTs of the chapters we have learnt. The computer lab is in the basement of the school.
